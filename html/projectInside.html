This home, a seamless fusion of the homeowners' personal preferences, is a reflection of their lives—certain corners bearing the mark of Polish experience, while others embody the aesthetics of Taiwan. The female homeowner hails from Poland, and the male homeowner from Taiwan. From the earliest stages of design, their discussions sparked many exciting ideas, such as the openness to seeing the fireplace upon entry or the flexible connections and proportional balance between the living room, dining room, and bedroom.
